Quick Chicken Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, sliced
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and chicken, cook until browned.
  3. Add vegetables and soy sauce; stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Serve hot with rice or noodles.

Quinoa & Avocado Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, avocado, tomatoes, and red onion.
  2. Drizzle with lemon juice and toss gently.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Chill for 15 minutes before serving.

Chocolate Lava Cake

Ingredients:

  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 100g butter
  • 150g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 50g flour

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (392°F).
  2. Melt chocolate and butter in a bowl.
  3. Whisk in sugar and eggs until smooth.
  4. Fold in flour gently.
  5. Pour into greased ramekins and bake for 10-12 minutes.
  6. Serve warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
